Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Hidden Orient Lore ("we", "our", "us") operates an automated content publishing platform for educational history content across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services.

Information We Collect

Our application collects and processes the following information:

  • Account Authentication Data: OAuth tokens required to publish content to your connected social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram). We do not store your passwords.
  • Analytics Data: Publicly available video performance metrics (views, likes, comments) from your published content to optimize future content strategy.

How We Use Information

  • To publish video content to authorized social media platforms on your behalf
  • To analyze content performance and improve content strategy
  • To send notifications about publishing status via Telegram

Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device in encrypted format. We do not transmit or store your credentials on external servers. All data processing happens locally.

Third-Party Services

Our application interacts with the following third-party APIs:

  • YouTube Data API v3 (Google)
  • TikTok Content Posting API
  • Instagram Graph API (Meta)
  • ElevenLabs API (text-to-speech)
  • Leonardo.ai API (image generation)
  • Telegram Bot API (notifications)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to the social media platforms you have explicitly authorized.

Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained as long as they are valid. You can revoke access at any time by removing the application from your social media account settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to any connected platform at any time
  • Request deletion of locally stored data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our YouTube channel: Hidden Orient Lore.
